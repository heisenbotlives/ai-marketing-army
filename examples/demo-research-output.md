# Demo Research Output

## Topic

How AI agents can support marketing teams.

## Summary

AI agents are most useful in marketing when they help with repeatable workflows: source monitoring, research synthesis, brief creation, content drafting, and review. The core opportunity is not replacing marketers, but giving marketers more leverage across research and production.

## Key observations

- Many teams start with content drafting, but research and synthesis may be more defensible use cases.
- Human review remains important because AI can hallucinate claims and flatten brand voice.
- A knowledge base improves results by giving agents reusable context.
- Specialist agents are easier to evaluate than one general-purpose assistant.

## Marketing implications

A strong message should avoid "fully autonomous marketing" hype and instead focus on human-led systems, review loops, and practical leverage.

## Confidence

Medium. This is a demonstration note, not a full market study.
