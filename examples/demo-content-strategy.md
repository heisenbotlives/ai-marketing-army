# Demo Content Strategy

## Recommendation

Position the AI Marketing Army as a practical operating model for AI-enabled marketers: a way to combine judgement, research, content production, and review without pretending the system is fully autonomous.

## Messaging pillars

1. **Leverage, not replacement** — AI agents help one marketer operate with more reach.
2. **Systems, not prompts** — the durable value is workflow design and knowledge management.
3. **Human review matters** — quality gates prevent hallucinations and generic output.
4. **Public proof of capability** — the repo shows practical AI fluency to employers.

## Content angles

- "The next marketing skill is designing AI workflows, not just writing prompts."
- "Why your AI marketing stack needs a knowledge base before it needs more tools."
- "How I’m building an AI-assisted marketing operating system as a portfolio project."
